Malin Kundang

Malin Kundang is a folk tale from West Sumatra about a poor young man who goes away to seek fortune and becomes rich. When he returns, he is ashamed to recognize his poor mother. In anger, his mother curses him, and Malin Kundang turns into stone. The story teaches the importance of respecting parents.

Timun Mas

The story of Timun Mas tells about a deal made between Mbok Randa and a giant regarding Mbok Randa's wish for a child. Because of this agreement, Timun Mas is born. However, Mbok Randa breaks the promise because she cannot bear to give Timun Mas to the giant.

Bawang Merah Bawang Putih

The story of Cinderella in Europe tells about two stepsisters with very different personalities: Bawang Putih, who is kind and hardworking, and Bawang Merah, who is lazy and envious.

Roro Jonggrang

The legend tells of a princess who requires Bandung Bondowoso to build a thousand temples in one night to marry her. After he tricks her by faking dawn, he curses her to become a stone statue, which explains the origin of Prambanan Temple.

Sangkuriang

The West Javanese legend tells the story of Sangkuriang, who falls in love with his mother, Dayang Sumbi. When he learns her true identity, Sangkuriang becomes angry. To avoid marrying him, Dayang Sumbi gives him a challenge: he must build a boat in one night. When he is close to finishing, Dayang Sumbi stops him, and in his frustration, Sangkuriang kicks the boat over, which then turns into Mount Tangkuban Perahu

Danau Toba

Toba, a farmer, marries a magical fish who turns into a beautiful woman. When he calls their son Samosir "fish child," his wife returns to the sea, creating Lake Toba with Samosir as an island. In another tale, Princess Sari is cursed to be a snail. A prince falls in love with her, learns her identity, and fights to break the curse, restoring her form so they can marry, emphasizing love and loyalty

Keong Mas

A Javanese folktale about Sari, a princess cursed to become a snail. A prince unknowingly falls in love with her, and after learning her identity, he fights to break the curse. He restores her form, and they marry, emphasizing love and loyalty.

Batu Menangis

A story about a princess who rejects a prince's love and is turned into stone. Her tears then form a lake, becoming a symbol of sadness and regret.

Lutung Kasarung

The story of Lutung Kasarung is about a lutung (a type of monkey) who is actually a cursed prince. He tries to win the love of a princess with the help of magical powers and the challenges he must face.
